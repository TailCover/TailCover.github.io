Privacy Policy

Cover Your Tails respects your privacy and is designed to collect and use only the information needed to provide the app’s functionality.

Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

• Your phone number and basic account information
• Phone numbers and names of contacts you choose to add
• Check-in schedules and message content you configure
• Optional location data, if you enable location features

How information is used

Information is used solely to:

• Deliver scheduled check-ins to you
• Send notifications or text messages when check-ins are missed, based on your pre-configured settings
• Support optional features you choose to enable

Location data

Location tracking is optional and disabled by default. If you enable location features, location data may be used to support app functionality you opt into.

Location information is not shared with your contacts unless you explicitly configure the app to do so.

Consent and messaging

Messages to contacts are sent only when you miss check-ins and only according to the rules and message content you set in advance.

Contacts are notified during setup that they have been added as a Cover Your Tails contact.

Data sharing

Cover Your Tails does not sell personal data. Information is shared only with service providers required to deliver messaging and app functionality.

Data retention

Data is retained only as long as needed to provide the service and may be deleted upon account removal.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@coveryourtails.com.
